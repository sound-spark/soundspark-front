RECOGNITION

There are several ASR solutions on the market right now but there are still many gaps to fill. As voice and speech recognition market is expected to grow even faster now, we decided to build our own solution from scratch.

Our goal is to create a working ASR for the Polish language while testing different approaches to understand their advantages and their shortcomings.

Our success criteria is to achieve Word Error Rate on level ~5% for the clean speech.

We are just at the beginning of our journey-for this reason at this point of time we use only open-sourced data for testing and our home computation resources.

END‑TO‑END

The first approach that we tested was an End-to-End. There are several E2E working solutions currently on the market. Their pros is that they are fairly easy to design. On the other hand, they need a huge amount of data and computation resources to train a working model.

On the left side you can see an image with model architecture that was trained. We used MFCC as speech features. We used CTC technique to match speech input frames into output labels.

Even though keras method 'fit_generator' was used to minimise memory consumption needed for training process, it was still crushing because of 'out of memory' error. Training had to be divided into several turns of 5 epochs each.

As we knew that this solution would not produce the best results, we finished this experiment at this stage.
