There are several ASR solutions on the market right now but still there are many gaps to fill. As voice and speech recognition market is expected to grow even faster now, we decided to build our own solution step by step.

Our goal is to create working ASR for polish language and explore different approaches. Working means to achieve Word Error Rate on level ~5% for clean speech.

Our approach is to make the best we can not investing any money, that means we use only open-sourced data and only our home computation resources.

There are several E2E working solution on the market. Their pros is that they are fairly easy to design. On the other hand they need a huge amount of data and computation resources to train working model.

With 52 hours of clean speech to work with and laptop, we didn't expect to achieve good performance. Only 32 hours of speech was used for training. The rest of data was used for test and validation. On the left side you can see image with model architecture that was trained. As speech features we used MFCC. To match speech input frames into output labels we used CTC technique. Even though keras method 'fit_generator' was used to minimize memory consumption needed for training process, it was crushing because of 'out of memory' error. Training had to be divided into several turns of 5 epochs each.

As we knew that this solution will not be the best we finished this experiment at this stage. You can give it a try here
